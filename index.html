UC: National Parks

Brazil is well known for its natural beauty and rich biodiversity. And to protect it, Brazil has UCs (Unidades de Conservação - "Conservation Unity"), protected areas by brazilian law. The objectives of UC include conservation of nature, sustainable development, scientific research, education and eco-tourism.

There are 12 types of UCs and National Parks is one type.
